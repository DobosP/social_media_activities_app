Privacy Policy

Last reviewed: draft. This platform is operated for users in Romania and the EU and is designed around the GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and the Digital Services Act.

Who we are

This service is a nonprofit, text-first platform for arranging in-person activities. The operating organisation is the data controller for the personal data described below. (Legal entity name, address and registration number to be inserted by legal.)

What data we collect

  • Age band, not date of birth. We only ever store an age band (for example "over 16" or "over 18"). We do not collect or store your birthdate. Age is proven through the EU Digital Identity wallet, which discloses only the band to us.
  • Username and display name you choose at registration. We do not require your real name.
  • Activity data you create or take part in: activities you organise or join, posts in activity threads, interests you select, places and events you view, and membership/voting records needed to run the activity.
  • Safety data needed to keep minors and the community safe: reports you file or that concern you, blocks, and moderation decisions.
  • Account security data such as a hashed password and basic technical logs.

We do not run behavioural advertising, sell personal data, or profile you to manipulate engagement.

Lawful basis for processing

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) to provide the account and activity features you ask for.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) for age assurance and child-safety duties, including obligations under the Digital Services Act.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) to keep the service safe, prevent abuse, and maintain security — balanced against your rights.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) where specifically requested. For users under 16, processing relies on parental/guardian consent as required by GDPR Art. 8 and Romanian law.

How long we keep data (retention)

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, then delete or anonymise it. Account data is retained while your account is active. Safety and moderation records may be retained longer where required to protect users or to meet legal obligations. (Concrete retention periods to be confirmed by the DPO.)

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you and obtain a copy.
  • Rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten", Art. 17). You can delete your account and personal data yourself from your {% if user.is_authenticated %}profile page{% else %}profile page{% endif %}, or by contacting our Data Protection Officer.
  • Restriction and objection to certain processing.
  • Data portability for data you provided to us.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in Romania the National Supervisory Authority for Personal Data Processing (ANSPDCP).

Children and guardians

This platform is used by minors. Users under 16 require verified parental or guardian consent before they can join or organise activities, and we keep cohorts age-separated. Guardians can review and withdraw consent at any time.
