Meet people. Do real things. In person.

A nonprofit, text-first platform for organising in-person activities — sport, outdoor, reading, board & video games, culture — at real places near you. No public photo feeds, no endless scrolling, no ads.

Get started Browse places

How it works

1. Find a place & people

Places are already on the map (courts, parks, libraries, cafes). Tell us your interests and we suggest activities for you nearby.

2. Join by vote

Ask to join an activity; current members vote you in (two-thirds by default). The group decides who joins.

3. Organise & chat

Create your own meetup, post in its private thread, share photos, and chat live with members — text-first, always.

Safe by design: people are grouped into age cohorts and only ever see and join activities within their own cohort — children interact only with similar-age peers. Age checks are simulated in this demo.
